## 1. Information We Collect

### Account Information
When you create an account, we collect:
- Email address and display name (Email/Password sign-in)
- Google account name and email (Google Sign-In)
- A randomly assigned name for Guest (anonymous) accounts

### Health & Fitness Data
- **Step count** — recorded continuously in the background via your device's step sensor
- **Walk sessions** — GPS route, distance, duration, and calories burned during active walks
- **Health metrics** — height, weight, age, and sex (entered by you during profile setup), used to calculate BMI, Health Age, and estimated VO₂ Max
- **Heart rate** — read from Health Connect (if you grant permission), used only to display resting heart rate in Insights. Never uploaded.

### Location Data
- **Precise location** is collected during active walks only, to draw your route on the map
- Location is not collected in the background when you are not on an active walk
- Route coordinates are stored locally and synced to your account in Firebase

### Device Data
- **FCM token** — stored to send you push notifications (group invites, join requests)

## 2. How We Use Your Information

| Data | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Steps & walk history | Show your daily progress, weekly trends, and leaderboard rank |
| GPS route | Draw route map on walk completion screen |
| Profile (height, weight, age) | Calculate health metrics shown in Insights |
| Display name | Show in community leaderboard and group screens |
| Email | Account authentication and recovery |
| FCM token | Send group notifications |

---

## 3. Data Sharing

We **do not sell** your personal data.

Your **display name and step count** are visible to other users in:
- The global leaderboard
- Groups you are a member of

All other data (GPS routes, health metrics, profile details) is **private to your account only**.

---

## 4. Data Storage

Your data is stored using:
- **Firebase Firestore** (Google LLC) — account profile, step records, group data, notifications
- **Local device storage** (Room database) — step history and walk sessions cached on your device

Firebase is GDPR-compliant. Data is stored on Google Cloud servers.
Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

---

## 5. Health Connect

If you grant Health Connect permission, Chalo Walk Par reads:
- Heart rate (to display resting heart rate in Insights)
- Steps (to cross-reference with sensor data)

This data is **read-only**, never modified, and never uploaded to our servers.
You can revoke Health Connect permissions at any time in Android Settings → Health Connect.

---

## 6. Guest (Anonymous) Accounts

If you sign in as a Guest:
- No email is collected
- Step data and walk history are stored locally only and are **not synced** to the cloud
- Your data is permanently deleted when you sign out or uninstall the app

---

## 7. Data Retention & Deletion

- You can delete your account at any time from the Profile screen → Sign Out
- To request permanent deletion of all your data from our servers, email us at prabuddhaverma2548@gmail.com
- We will delete your data within 30 days of your request

## 8. Children's Privacy

Chalo Walk Par is intended for users **18 years and older**. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.
