Privacy Policy — Chalo Walk Par

Last updated: July 14, 2026
Developer: Prabuddha
Contact: chalowalkpar@gmail.com

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address and display name (Email/Password sign-in)
  • Google account name and email (Google Sign-In)
  • A randomly assigned name for Guest (anonymous) accounts

Health & Fitness Data

  • Step count — recorded via your device's step sensor
  • Walk sessions — GPS route, distance, duration, and calories burned during active walks
  • Health metrics — height, weight, age, sex entered by you during profile setup
  • Heart rate — read from Health Connect if you grant permission. Never uploaded to our servers.

Location Data

  • Precise location is collected during active walks only to draw your route on the map
  • Location is not collected in the background when you are not on an active walk

Device Data

  • FCM token — stored to send push notifications for group invites and join requests

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Your display name and step count are visible to other users in the global leaderboard and groups you are a member of. All other data is private to your account only.

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored using Firebase Firestore (Google LLC) for account profile, step records, group data, and notifications, and locally on your device for step history and walk sessions.

Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Health Connect

If you grant Health Connect permission, Chalo Walk Par reads heart rate and steps for display in Insights only. This data is read-only and never uploaded to our servers. You can revoke permissions anytime in Android Settings → Health Connect.

6. Guest (Anonymous) Accounts

No email is collected for guest accounts. Data is stored locally only and is not synced to the cloud. All data is permanently deleted when you sign out or uninstall the app.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

You can delete your account from Profile → Sign Out. To request permanent deletion of all your data, email chalowalkpar@gmail.com. We will delete your data within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

Chalo Walk Par is intended for users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
